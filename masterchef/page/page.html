Lavender Milk Tea Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Culinary lavender, Dried
  • 1 Honey or sugar
  • 1 Vanilla extract
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup steamed milk
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew a fresh cup of coffee.
  2. Froth the milk and pour it into the coffee.
  3. Sweeten with sugar and sprinkle cinnamon if desired.

Cocktails

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • Ice
  • Fresh mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Combine vodka, lime juice, and simple syrup in a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a glass.
  3. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve chilled.
